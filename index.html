Hyderabad

Hyderabad is my favorite city because it perfectly mixes old and new and also it is also my Hometown. It has amazing historical sites like the Charminar and Golconda Fort. The food is incredible, especially the famous biryani and haleem . The city is also growing fast with its tech businesses and modern lifestyle. With its busy markets and peaceful lakes, Hyderabad feels like a place where the past and present come together in a wonderful way.
